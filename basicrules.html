Football Rules

The beautiful game's essential regulations

Basic Gameplay

  • 11 players per team on the field
  • 90 minutes total (2 halves of 45 minutes)
  • Only goalkeepers can handle the ball
  • Offside rule applies when attacking

Scoring

  • Goal = 1 point (entire ball must cross line)
  • No points for near misses
  • Own goals count for opposing team

Fouls & Penalties

  • Yellow card = warning
  • Red card = immediate ejection
  • Penalty kick for fouls in the box
  • Free kicks for other fouls

Basketball Rules

Fast-paced court action fundamentals

Basic Gameplay

  • 5 players per team on court
  • 4 quarters of 12 minutes (NBA)
  • Shot clock: 24 seconds to attempt shot
  • Dribble required to move with ball

Scoring

  • 3 points for shots beyond arc
  • 2 points for regular field goals
  • 1 point for free throws

Violations

  • Traveling (illegal movement)
  • Double dribble
  • 3-second lane violation
  • Backcourt violation

Cricket Rules

The gentleman's game simplified

Basic Gameplay

  • Two teams of 11 players
  • Batting and bowling sides alternate
  • Runs scored by running between wickets or hitting boundaries
  • Overs limit varies by format (Test, ODI, T20)

Scoring

  • 4 runs for boundary, 6 runs for over boundary
  • Runs added for successful running
  • Extras: wides, no-balls, byes, leg byes

Dismissals

  • Bowled, Caught, LBW, Run-out, Stumped
  • 10 wickets ends the batting innings

Tennis Rules

Serve, rally, and score with precision

Basic Gameplay

  • Singles (1v1) or Doubles (2v2)
  • Alternate serving each game
  • Ball must land inside designated court area
  • First to win 6 games (with 2-game margin) wins a set

Scoring

  • Points: 15, 30, 40, Game
  • Deuce at 40-40, then advantage needed to win
  • Matches typically best of 3 or 5 sets

Violations

  • Double fault (lose point on serve)
  • Foot faults (stepping on line during serve)
  • Ball out of bounds = point to opponent

Hockey Rules

Fast and fierce field battles

Basic Gameplay

  • 11 players per team on the field
  • Match consists of 4 quarters (15 minutes each)
  • Ball moved with stick; only flat side allowed

Scoring

  • Goal scored when ball crosses line inside goalposts
  • Only inside shooting circle goals are valid

Fouls

  • High stick (dangerous play)
  • Obstruction or body contact is penalized
  • Green card = warning, Yellow = temporary suspension, Red = expulsion

Swimming Rules

Stroke your way to the finish

Basic Gameplay

  • Freestyle, Breaststroke, Backstroke, Butterfly races
  • Start from blocks; underwater phase varies by stroke
  • No false starts allowed

Scoring

  • First swimmer to touch wall wins
  • Relay events combine times of all swimmers

Violations

  • Early start disqualifies swimmer
  • Incorrect stroke technique may lead to disqualification

Badminton Rules

Fast-paced shuttle action

Basic Gameplay

  • Singles or Doubles matches
  • Best of 3 games to 21 points
  • Serve must be below waist height

Scoring

  • Rally scoring system (point scored every rally)
  • Win by 2 points margin if tied at 20-20

Violations

  • Shuttle landing outside court = point to opponent
  • Touching net = fault

Golf Rules

Precision, patience, and strategy

Basic Gameplay

  • 18 holes standard course
  • Lowest strokes total wins
  • Play ball as it lies

Scoring

  • Par: standard strokes for hole
  • Birdie: 1 under par, Bogey: 1 over par
  • Hole-in-one: completing hole in single shot

Penalties

  • Stroke penalties for rule violations
  • Out-of-bounds: stroke and distance penalty

Kabaddi Rules

Skillful raids and tackles

Basic Gameplay

  • 7 players on court per team
  • Raiders must touch opponents and return
  • Chant "Kabaddi" without inhaling during raid

Scoring

  • Each touch-out earns 1 point
  • All-out bonus for getting entire team out

Violations

  • Breaking chant = raid ends
  • Illegal holds = points to opponent

Volleyball Rules

Spikes, blocks, and teamwork

Basic Gameplay

  • 6 players per team on court
  • Three touches per side before sending ball over
  • Rotate positions clockwise after winning serve

Scoring

  • Rally scoring (point every rally)
  • First to 25 points wins set (lead by 2)
  • Best of 5 sets to win match

Violations

  • Touching net is a fault
  • Double hits or lifts not allowed
  • Ball landing outside court = point to opponent
